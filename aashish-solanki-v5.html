About Aashish

Aashish Solanki is one of India's most celebrated stand-up comedians, known for razor-sharp observational humour about family, relationships, and the beautiful chaos of Indian middle-class life.

From his breakout special Papa In Police to hosting two legendary roast series, Aashish has built a comedy empire — and somehow made lying feel like an art form.

The Journey

Early Days
Open mics & Mumbai beginnings
Started performing in Mumbai's comedy circuit, honing the observational style about Indian family life that would make him famous.
Breakthrough
Papa In Police 🚔
The special that went viral. A story about the most dramatic papa in Indian comedy history — and the call that started everything.
Roast Era
Pretty Good Roast Show & BroCode
